abstract: >-
  In this paper, an improved Wasserstein generative adversal network with gradient penalty (WGAN-GP) is proposed to solve the channel modeling and generation problem in MIMO communication system. The experimental results show that the improved WGAN-GP can generate fake channel data with the same distribution as the real channel data.
